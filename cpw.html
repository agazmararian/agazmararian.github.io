Climate Policy Workshop

About

CPW is an interdisciplinary space at U-M for faculty to workshop their social science research on climate change policy and politics, broadly defined.

Our aims are twofold: 1) foster U-M's community of social scientists working on climate change, and 2) provide a venue for feedback on faculty research in progress.

Many of these conversations are already happening in pockets across campus; our intention is to create a complementary institution that brings together people who are studying similar questions but might not yet realize their common climate interests.

Workshop attendance is open to faculty, postdocs, PhD students, and master's students.
