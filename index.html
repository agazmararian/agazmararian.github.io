Alex is a Ph.D. student in the Department of Politics at Princeton University. He researches the political economy of climate change, in addition to topics in international security. 

At Princeton, he is a student associate at the Niehaus Center for Globalization and Governance. 

Previously, he worked as the Research Specialist at the Policy Resolution Group, a government relations and strategic communications firm in Washington, D.C. 

He holds a B.A. in Political Science with highest honors from Emory University.
