About

Alex is a Ph.D. student in the Department of Politics at Princeton University. His research interests include interstate conflict, coercion, climate change, and more.

At Princeton, he is a student associate at the Niehaus Center for Globalization and Governance.

Previously, he worked as the Research Director at the Policy Resolution Group, a government relations and strategic communications firm in Washington, D.C.

He holds a B.A. in Political Science with highest honors from Emory University.
