Alex is a Ph.D. student in Politics at Princeton University. He studies political violence, applying quantitative methods to better understand the logic and consequences of conflict between and within states.

Previously, Alex worked as the Research Director at the Policy Resolution Group in Washington, D.C. He completed his Bachelor of Arts in Political Science with Highest Honors at Emory University, where he also coached for and competed on their top-five nationally ranked policy debate team.
